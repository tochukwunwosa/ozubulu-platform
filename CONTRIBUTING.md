# Contributing to the Ozubulu Digital Platform

Thank you for helping preserve the history and future of Ozubulu. Every contribution matters.

## You Can Contribute

* Historical information
* Old photographs
* Documents
* Community news
* Festival information
* Stories
* Corrections
* Business information
* School history
* Church history

## Before You Submit

* Make sure your information is truthful.
* Make sure you have permission to share it.
* Double-check dates and names.
* Include sources where possible.

## What We Do Not Accept

* Hate speech
* Personal attacks
* Political propaganda
* Rumours
* Defamatory content
* Copyright violations
* Fake information

## Review Process

Submission → Review → Verification → Editing → Publication → Future Updates

Every submission is reviewed in accordance with the [Editorial Policy](docs/02-editorial-policy.md) before publication.

## Writing Standards

Please follow the [Content Standards Guide](docs/04-content-standards.md) for writing style, tone, sourcing, and accessibility when preparing content for submission.

## Full Guide

For the complete contributor guide, including recognition and review details, see [docs/05-contributor-guide.md](docs/05-contributor-guide.md).

Thank you for being part of this journey.
