# Governance Charter

*Ozubulu Digital Platform*

## Purpose

The Ozubulu Digital Platform exists to serve the people of Ozubulu. No individual, family, village, organisation, or sponsor is greater than the mission of preserving the history, culture, and identity of our community. Every decision made for this platform should protect that mission.

## Founder

The Ozubulu Digital Platform was founded by Nwosa Tochukwu Chinemelu. The founder is responsible for:

* Product vision
* Technical development
* Platform security
* Infrastructure
* Editorial leadership during Version 1

## Future Governance

The long-term vision is for this platform to become a trusted community resource. When appropriate, governance may expand to include:

* Traditional Institution
* Ozubulu Town Union
* Village Representatives
* Religious Representatives
* Education Representatives
* Youth Representatives
* Diaspora Representatives

This transition will only happen when it strengthens the platform.

## Decision Making

Every important decision should answer three questions:

* Does it preserve Ozubulu's history?
* Does it benefit the community?
* Will it still make sense ten years from now?

If the answer is "No" to any of these questions, the decision should be reconsidered.

## Independence

Editorial decisions shall never be influenced by:

* Money
* Politics
* Personal relationships
* Family interests
* Tribal rivalry
* Religious bias

The platform serves everyone equally.

## Transparency

Major platform decisions should be documented. Important updates should be communicated publicly. Community trust depends on openness.

## Stewardship

This platform should outlive its founder. Every system should be designed so future generations can continue the work without starting again. That is our responsibility.
