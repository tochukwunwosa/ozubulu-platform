# Contributor Guide

*Ozubulu Digital Platform*

## Welcome

Thank you for helping preserve the history and future of Ozubulu. Every contribution matters.

## You Can Contribute

We welcome:

* Historical information
* Old photographs
* Documents
* Community news
* Festival information
* Stories
* Corrections
* Business information
* School history
* Church history

## Before You Submit

Please make sure:

* Your information is truthful.
* You have permission to share it.
* Dates and names are correct.
* Sources are included where possible.

## What We Do Not Accept

We do not publish:

* Hate speech
* Personal attacks
* Political propaganda
* Rumours
* Defamatory content
* Copyright violations
* Fake information

## Review Process

Every submission follows the same process:

Submission → Review → Verification → Editing → Publication → Future Updates

## Recognition

Whenever appropriate, contributors will be acknowledged for their work. History belongs to the community. Everyone who helps preserve it deserves recognition.

## Thank You

By contributing to this platform, you are helping preserve the story of Ozubulu for future generations. Your contribution may become part of our community's history. Thank you for being part of this journey.
