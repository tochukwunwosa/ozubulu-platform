# ADR-005: Community Contributions Require Editorial Review

**Status:** Accepted

## Context

The platform welcomes community participation but must maintain historical accuracy and editorial quality.

## Decision

All community contributions will be reviewed before publication.

## Rationale

Editorial review protects the integrity of the platform while encouraging responsible participation from the community.

## Consequences

No public submission is published automatically. Every accepted contribution becomes part of the platform only after editorial approval.
