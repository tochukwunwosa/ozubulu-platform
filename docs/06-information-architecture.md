# Information Architecture (Version 1)

*Ozubulu Digital Platform*

## Purpose

The Information Architecture defines how information is organised across the Ozubulu Digital Platform. Its purpose is to ensure that every visitor can easily find information, every contributor knows where content belongs, and every future feature has a logical place within the platform.

A good structure makes the platform easy to use today and easy to grow tomorrow.

## Design Principles

The structure of the platform is guided by five principles:

### Simple

Visitors should find information with as few clicks as possible.

### Organized

Related information should always live together. Nothing should feel random or out of place.

### Scalable

The platform should be able to grow from dozens of pages to hundreds, and eventually thousands, without becoming confusing.

### Searchable

Every important piece of information should be discoverable through navigation and search.

### Community First

The platform is designed around the needs of the Ozubulu community—not around technology.

## Primary Navigation

The main navigation will remain simple. Large sections will use Mega Menus to organise related content. The primary navigation consists of:

* Home
* Discover
* Heritage
* Community
* Support

## Home

The Home page introduces visitors to Ozubulu and provides quick access to the most important areas of the platform. It includes:

* Hero Section
* Welcome Message
* Featured Stories
* Community Highlights
* Community Updates
* Upcoming Events
* Quick Links
* Search
* Featured Gallery
* Support the Project

The Home page should always answer one question: "Where should I start?"

## Information Hierarchy

Every piece of information belongs somewhere. The hierarchy is:

```
Platform
  └─ Section
       └─ Category
            └─ Content Type
                 └─ Individual Page
                      └─ Related Content
```

This structure keeps information organised, connected, and easy to discover.

## Guiding Principle

Every page should answer a real question that someone might ask about Ozubulu. If a page does not educate, preserve, connect, or serve the community, it does not belong on the platform.

The Information Architecture exists to ensure that every visitor can explore Ozubulu with clarity, confidence, and curiosity.
