# Ozubulu Digital Platform

# Project Roadmap

**Version:** 1.0

**Status:** Living Document

---

# Purpose

This roadmap outlines the planned phases of the Ozubulu Digital Platform from planning through public launch and future growth.

It provides a clear direction for the project while allowing flexibility as new information, opportunities, and community needs emerge.

The roadmap is intended to guide development, content creation, community engagement, and long-term stewardship.

---

# Guiding Principles

Every phase of the project should uphold the platform's core values:

* Truth before speed.
* Documentation before implementation.
* Community before complexity.
* Quality before quantity.
* Long-term preservation over short-term convenience.

# Risks

Potential risks include:

* Limited historical sources.
* Conflicting historical accounts.
* Delays in content gathering.
* Funding constraints.
* Limited contributor availability.
* Technical complexity.
* Scope creep.

Each risk should be documented, monitored, and addressed throughout the project.

---

# Success Metrics

Within the first year after launch, the project aims to:

* Publish at least **100 verified pages**.
* Maintain verification information for all historical articles.
* Process community corrections within **14 days** where possible.
* Receive contributions from community members and the Ozubulu diaspora.
* Achieve recognition as a trusted digital resource for Ozubulu.

These metrics may evolve as the platform grows.

# Guiding Principle

The Ozubulu Digital Platform is a long-term commitment to preserving the history and identity of a community.

Progress should be measured not only by features delivered, but by the quality, trustworthiness, and lasting value of the knowledge preserved.

Every completed phase brings Ozubulu one step closer to a permanent digital legacy.
