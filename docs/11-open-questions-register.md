# Open Questions Register

*Ozubulu Digital Platform — Internal*

## Purpose

An archive that only shows what it has found can quietly mislead readers: silence on a topic can look like "there was nothing to say" when the truth is "we asked and could not find out." This register is the counterpart — a permanent, internal record of specific historical questions we have actively investigated and still cannot answer.

It exists so that:

* Future contributors don't waste effort re-asking questions that already hit a dead end, without at least knowing what was already tried.
* We can be honest, in our own internal record, about the real limits of what's currently knowable — separate from what's merely unpublished.
* Genuine gaps stay visible instead of quietly disappearing when a phase of content entry moves on.

This register is internal. Whether and how any of it is ever surfaced to readers (for example, as a public "help us find out" page) is a future decision, not implied by this document existing.

## What belongs here — and what doesn't

**Belongs here:** a specific question was put to a named source — an informant, a traditional institution, a document search, a records office — and the answer came back unknown, contradictory beyond reconciliation, or permanently unavailable (records destroyed, no living witness, etc.).

**Does not belong here:**

* Outstanding items awaiting a *first* answer. Those live in the per-community staging files under `docs/content-staging/` (e.g. `questions-for-informants.md`). Move an item here only after a real attempt has failed — not simply because it hasn't been asked yet.
* Ordinary multi-account disputes that the Editorial Policy's [Handling Disputes](02-editorial-policy.md#handling-disputes) process already covers by presenting multiple accounts. Only cross-link a dispute here if, on top of that, there's no visible path to ever narrowing it further — so future contributors know not to keep re-litigating it.
* Anything simply not yet entered into Sanity. That's tracked in `docs/content-staging/`, not here.

## Entry format

```
### <Short question>

- **Why it matters:** what this blocks or affects (an article, a person's biography, a lineage claim, etc.)
- **What we tried:** who was asked / what was searched, and when
- **Status:** Conflicting accounts | No surviving record | Community declined to answer | Awaiting a rare/specific source | Lost to time
- **Opened:** YYYY-MM-DD
- **Last revisited:** YYYY-MM-DD
- **Linked content:** affected Sanity documents (e.g. `person.xxx`, `community.xxx`) or staging files
```

## Review cadence

"Cannot answer" is a status, not a life sentence. Revisit this list whenever a new informant becomes available, roughly every 6 months otherwise, and whenever a related staging file is touched. If a question is answered, move the entry into the relevant `docs/content-staging/*.md` file (or straight into Sanity) and remove it from here.
